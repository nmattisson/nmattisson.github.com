Nils Mattisson.

Founder of Form. We make intelligent things that look after you and your home. We're really just getting started, so sign up to our newsletter to follow along on our journey.

I'm a Product Designer and Engineer from Sweden. Upon defending my thesis and graduating from Lund University I moved to San Francisco to take a position in the Exploratory Design Group at Apple. We worked in the early stages of product development, making prototypes of new hardware concepts and features.

I stayed with Apple for about seven years but eventually got the startup itch which led me to move to Barcelona. I started working on Form out of Betahaus, where I found a great community of like-minded people.

I enjoy being outdoors, preferably in the mountains, riding bicycles, hiking and taking photographs.
