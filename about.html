Nils Mattisson.

I'm a Product Designer and Engineer from Sweden. Upon defending my thesis and graduating from Lund University I moved to San Francisco to take a position in the Exploratory Design Group at Apple. We worked in the early stages of product development, making prototypes of new hardware concepts and features.

I stayed with Apple for about seven years but eventually got the startup itch which led me to move to Barcelona where I am now working out of Betahaus, building something new for the Internet of Things.

When not working, I enjoy being outdoors, preferably in the mountains, riding bicycles, hiking and taking photographs.
